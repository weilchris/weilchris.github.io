  • Biography

    Chris Weil (*March 2, 1988, Bad Nauheim) is a German filmmaker and multimedia artist, who currently lives and works in Italy. After graduating as a Film & Video Editor in 2011 at the ARD.ZDF Media Academy in association with the Bayerischer Rundfunk and the IHK, Christian Tobias Weil starts video editing for the German public broadcasting stations (ARD and BR).

    In 2015, Chris Weil moves to Italy and, in collaboration with Sara Ferro, founds the creative directors collective ARTOLDO. The collective produces a diverse range of works, including Arthouse movies, Feature Documentaries, Experimental Films, Moving Images Arts, Video Art, and Virtual Reality experiences.
    Notable works are C. G. Jung on Alchemy (2023), Ugo Dossi (2017), and The Ritman Library (2017), alongside interactive art installations such as Achtung Banditen and kaltcaldo or The Virtual Journey of the Mask and Zoroaster Superstar, which are showcased in solo and group exhibitions.
